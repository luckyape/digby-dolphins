Welcome to the Digby Dolphins Swim Team!

We are a community-based swim team dedicated to developing swimmers of all ages and abilities. Our focus is on technique, endurance, and having fun in the water.

About Us

The Digby Dolphins Swim Team was established to provide competitive swimming opportunities for youth in our community. We believe that swimming is not just a sport but a life skill that builds confidence, discipline, and teamwork.
